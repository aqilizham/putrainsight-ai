## Recommended NotebookLM Import Order

1. Academic rules, academic calendar, and academic advising sources.
2. Counselling and wellbeing sources.
3. Welfare and financial support sources.
4. Leadership, student development, SULAM, and PUTRAFLEX sources.
5. Jiwa Putra and Berilmu Berbakti values sources.
6. Official competition documents once the team obtains the public URL or PDF.

## Tagging Standard

Use short tags such as `academic-support`, `academic-advising`, `wellbeing`, `student-welfare`, `financial-support`, `student-development`, `career-development`, `service-learning`, `jiwa-putra`, and `competition`.
