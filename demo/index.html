Problem Statement

Student support often begins only after visible difficulty appears. PutraInsight shifts the approach toward proactive success by identifying growth opportunities early and connecting students with the right university support ecosystem.

Solution Overview

The platform analyzes anonymized academic and engagement indicators, produces a Student Success Score, and recommends mentors, support services, wellbeing resources, leadership opportunities, and a 30-day success plan.
